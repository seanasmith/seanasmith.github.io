iMovie Exercise

Learning Objectives

  • Practice shooting and editing video for the Web.
  • Learn to embed video on the Web.

Description:

Shoot and edit a short video (no more than 90 seconds) and embedd the video on a basic HTML page. Think about your topic for Project 3, and perhaps use this exercise to start working on the video component for that project.

Steps:

  1. Shoot at least one interview using the framing and composition techniques we discussed in class.
  2. Shoot B-roll clips. Remember, you can never have too much B-roll, so shoot more than you think you need.
  3. Edit the project using iMovie (or your preferred video editing software). Cover all cuts with B-roll clips.
  4. Have at least three clips, B-roll covering the interview video with interview audio still audible.
  5. At least one transition and one text/title effect.
  6. Upload the video to YouTube or another video hosting site that allows embedding.
  7. Embed that video in a simple HTML page with a title and brief desription of the vide

Examples

  • International Research Conference
  • TXST Innovation Lab at SXSW

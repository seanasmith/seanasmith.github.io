Graduate Student and Assistant

Welcome to my site. I am a graduate student and instructional assistant in the School of Journalism and Mass Communication at Texas State University concentrating in digital media. I recently graduated summa cum laude from Sam Houston State University with a BA in Mass Communication, concentrating in multiplatform journalism and minoring in political science.

As an instructional assistant, I will teach Web Design & Publishing and assist in the Media Innovation Lab in the Spring 2018 semester. I was also selected to take part in the annual SXTXSTATE project, where mass communication graduate students attend SXSW Interactive and cover the event digitally.
